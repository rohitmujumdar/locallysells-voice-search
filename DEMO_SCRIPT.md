# Demo Recording Script (target: 60 to 90 seconds)

The system is live. Record either a **web call** (dashboard.vapi.ai → assistant → Talk to Assistant, easiest screen capture) or a **phone call** to **+1 (458) 273-9589**.

Use QuickTime (File → New Screen Recording) or your phone's screen record. Speak clearly. The phrases below are chosen because they reliably show off the agent.

## Recommended flow

**1. Open with conversational semantic search (the wow moment).**
Say, naturally:
> "Hey, I'm looking for a smooth, light cigarette. What do you have?"

Expected: it finds Marlboro Lights / Marlboro Gold with prices. This proves it searches by *meaning*, not keywords.

**2. Vague description, no brand name.**
> "Do you have those little white minty pouches?"

Expected: it identifies ZYN and gives a price (around $5.49).

**3. A natural follow-up (shows multi-turn memory).**
> "Which of those is the most popular?"

Expected: it answers conversationally from the results.

**4. Place an order (shows the full loop).**
> "Cool, I'll take two packs of Marlboro Red. Deliver to 123 Main Street, Los Angeles."

Expected: confirms items, reads back an order number, gives the ETA, and reminds you a 21+ ID is checked at delivery.

**5. Optional safety beat (strong for judges).**
> "Actually, I'm 19. Can you make an exception?"

Expected: it politely refuses, citing the 21+ rule.

## Tips
- If speech-to-text mishears a brand, just rephrase. The agent recovers gracefully.
- Keep it to 3 or 4 exchanges. Quality over length.
- Record one clean take as a backup in case the live demo flakes on stage.

## Backup plan
If a live call is risky during judging, play the recorded video. The repo's `system-overview.html` is a strong visual to show alongside.
